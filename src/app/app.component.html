Game Of Life And Death

History

Based on Conway's game of life. Two players are evolving cells in order to kill all the opponent's cells.

At each turn, the cells are evolving applying the games of life principles:

  • A cell dies of solitude with less than 2 neighbors
  • A cell dies of surpopulation with more than 2 neighbors
  • A new cell is born with 3 neighbors around

Gameplay

  • Click on a existing cell to kill it
  • Click on empty cell to create a new cell.
    Then click on two of your cells to feed the new cell
  • Apply Life to end your turn
